About

 

Arranged in the immediate vicinity of the entrance, the Decorative Sector tries to convince the visitor of the aesthetic importance of the garden. Annual and perennial plants, trees and shrubs, vines, decorative leaves, flowers and fruits are grown here. Thus, the space groups about 500 species and varieties of plants that provide a special decoration all year round. Each plant has its own personality that can affect the style of the area where it is planted. The shapes and sizes highlighted by the colors, textures and scents of flowers and leaves, lifespan, flowering period, growth form are characteristics that imprint this personality, thus playing an important role in the aesthetics of the garden.
