Adopt a Tree

Do you want to adopt a tree and/or request a donation?

 

Donation requests must be submitted at least two months prior to the event in order to be considered. Due to the frequency and number of donation requests, donations are not guaranteed. Responses to requests will be made as quickly as possible, but please, no phone calls or emails. If you do not receive a response within 30 days, it is likely that your request cannot be honored this year, and the Garden welcomes you to try again next year. Thank you for your understanding.
