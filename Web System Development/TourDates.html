Tour Dates

In recent years, Wonderland     has held numerous outdoor exhibitions with various attractions. Along with natural elements such as different species of flowers, trees, there were also well-known painters and beginners. The surprise elements of the last exhibitions were represented by the live concerts of the orchestra that performed well-known songs in the specially arranged hall.
